# Privacy Policy for YouTube Subtitle Pro

**Last Updated:** January 8, 2026

## 1. Data Collection
**YouTube Subtitle Pro** does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal user data on external servers. All operations are performed locally within your browser.

## 2. Permissions Usage
- **Storage:** Used solely to save your local preferences (subtitle styles, fonts, and templates) on your device.
- **Host Permissions (YouTube):** Used to inject style scripts into the YouTube page to modify the appearance of subtitles. We do not read or store your browsing history.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This extension operates independently and does not communicate with any third-party analytics or tracking services.

## 4. Contact
If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
